LONDON. — A meeting will be held at the Crown Coffee House, 14, Leather-lane, Holborn, on Sunday afternoon, at three o’clock, to form a new locality. A discussion will be held every Tuesday evening, to commence at half-past eight o’clock.

VICTIM COMMITTEE. — This committee will meet for a final settlement of affairs at Mr. Knox's, Skinner-street, Snow-hill, on Wednesday evening next, March 27th, at eight o’clock. All persons holding tickets connected with the George White dinner, are requested to return the same, or the money, immediately.

A PUBLIC MEETING of the shareholders of the City of London Institution, will be held on Sunday morning, March 24th, at ten o’clock, at the hall, Turnagain-lane, to elect directors, and transact other important business.

A PUBLIC MEETING will be held on Sunday evening, March 24th, at the Chartist Hall, Turnagain-lane. Chair to be taken at seven o’clock.

SOUTH LONDON CHARTIST HALL. — The Chartists of the above locality are requested to meet on Sunday morning next, at ten o’clock. The shareholders of the above hall are requested to meet on Sunday afternoon, at three o’clock.

JAMES BRONTERRE O’BRIEN will lecture at the South London Chartist Hall, on Sunday evening next, at half past seven o’clock.

FEARGUS O’CONNOR, Esq., will lecture at the South London Chartist Hall, on Monday evening next, at seven o’clock.

A TEA PARTY AND BALL will take place at the South London Chartist Hall, on Easter Monday, in honour of the Executive.
